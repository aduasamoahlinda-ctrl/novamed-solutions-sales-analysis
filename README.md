NovaMed Solutions Sales Analysis (Power BI)

Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Sales and Customer Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI for NovaMed Solutions, a pharmaceutical distribution company.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights across revenue, customers, products, and geography to support data-driven business decisions.

Business Problem
NovaMed needed a way to:
- Monitor sales and profitability performance
- Identify high-value customers and segments
- Understand which products drive revenue
- Detect underperforming regions and products
- Reduce risks from revenue concentration

Solution
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables stakeholders to:
- Track KPIs in real time
- Explore trends across multiple dimensions
- Drill down into customer and product performance
- Make strategic, data-backed decisions

Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: $71.3M
- Profit: $58.5M
- Profit Margin: 82.0%
- Quantity Sold: 269K units
- Total Customers: 200
- Avg Revenue per Customer: $356.5K

Key Insights
Revenue & Profitability
- High profit margin (82%)
- Stable revenue trend

Customer Insights
- Revenue concentrated among few customers
- Preferred customers generate most value

Geographic Insights
- Canada is top-performing region
- Revenue concentrated geographically

Product Performance
- Few drugs dominate revenue
- Some products underperform significantly

Recommendations
- Diversify customer base
- Expand into new regions
- Focus on high-performing products
- Target younger customers
- Strengthen seller partnerships
